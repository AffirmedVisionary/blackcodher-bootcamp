About Me

I am a 33 year old mother of 4, currently living in Walsall, West Midlands. Never really had an easy life but 7 years ago I made a 5 year plan to better myself with an aim to become self sufficient. In the fourth year after sorting health issues I undertook an entrepreneurship and a be happy course. Together they jump-started everything I have going on today.

Big Dreams Clear Visions CIC

My pride and joy! BDCV started with me curating simple group brainstorming lunches and quickly evolved to me offering:

  • Business Coaching
  • Mentorship
  • Business Plan Assessor for the local college
  • Vision Board Workshops
  • Co-Working Space

I cant forget my favourite part of the CIC, Definitely our girls group called UR-Eyes (pronounced your eyes and you rise). A 6 week workshop where I teach the girls all about self love, affirmations, resilience, legacy planning, entrepreneurship and so much more! At the moment we are trialing an online social media type platform for under 21s. If it proves needed the skills from the Black Codher bootcamp will enable me to create the site on my own without the need to rely on platforms. The site will provide a safe space for expression while inspiring, educating and empowering the youth of today.

The Affirmed Visionary

Divination, Motivation, Transformation, My name is Charlene Grant and I AM THE AFFIRMED VISIONARY

This is how I introduce myself in my head when I meet people. LMAO! I love my intro just as much as I love what I do. Affirmed Visionary is my brand, my identities alias. It sums me up in two words, I cannot do anything normal or substandard, always thinking outside of the box and I have the ability to expand ideas into visions far bigger than dreams.

Really though, I consider myself an intelligent, creative entrepreneur with a huge social conscience. Always thinking of ways my skills can help the community or the next generation of heroines. Some of the things I do include:

  • Trustee for my local Home Start Walsall
  • Big Local partnership member
  • Branding including logos, flyers, banners etc
  • I have my own merch line
  • I personalise gift items
  • Stylised portraits
  • Founder of Big Dreams Clear Visions
  • Maths Tutor
  • Black Codher Student
